Jungle Hit empire is an independent record label based in Cameroon, Douala

We bring you the most original, independent music from african artists and beyond

  • Driven by a passion for diversity and a deep appreciation for cultural richness.
  • Blending traditional Cameroonian rhythms with contemporary influences to create a unique and captivating sound.

Services

Our services

Artist Development

Jungle hit empire Records guides artists on their journey to success through Artist Crescendo Development, focusing on image refinement, branding, public relations, long-term career planning, and industry navigation to help artists achieve their full potential and make their mark in the music world.

Legal and Business Affairs

Our Legal and Business Affairs team ensures that artists are protected and fairly compensated, handling contract negotiations, copyright protection, royalty collection, licensing agreements, and intellectual property rights management with precision and care.

Studio Access

We provides artists with exclusive access to empire Studios, a cutting-edge recording facility equipped with top-of-the-line equipment, experienced sound engineers, and renowned producers to bring the artist's musical vision to life

Spotlight Campaigns

Our dedicated marketing team at Jungle Empire Records crafts Spotlight Campaigns for each artist, tailored to their unique style and audience. From securing radio play to organizing intimate listening parties, we ensure that your music reaches the right ears at the right time.

Tours

Our Jungle empire Tours support artists in planning and executing successful tours, providing access to seasoned tour managers, booking agents, promotional assistance, transportation, and accommodation to ensure unforgettable live performances and fan engagement.

Sync Symphonies Licensing

Through Sync Symphonies Licensing, Jungle Empire Records helps artists license their music for placements in films, TV shows, commercials, and other media, expanding their reach and generating additional income through royalties and licensing opportunities.
